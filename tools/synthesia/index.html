About Synthesia

Synthesia turns prompts, scripts, documents, or slides into presenter-led videos using realistic AI avatars. It's designed for business use cases like training, onboarding, product explainers, and internal communications. Video Agents allow interactive, conversational videos.

Key Features

  • 120+ realistic AI avatars
  • 65+ video templates
  • Custom avatar creation
  • Video Agents for interactive training
  • 50+ languages supported
  • AI Playground (free Veo 3 and Sora 2 access)
  • Brand kit and collaboration tools

Pros

  • • Highly realistic avatars
  • • Strong for business training and onboarding
  • • Video Agents enable interactive learning
  • • Wide language support
  • • Consistent, on-brand output

Cons

  • • Not designed for cinematic storytelling
  • • Presenter-led format is structured (not dynamic)
  • • Higher price point for business use

Best For

Employee training, onboarding, product explainers, internal communications, multilingual content, and interactive role-play learning
