Grammarly vs QuillBot 2026: Which Writing Assistant Is Right for You?

When it comes to AI writing assistants, Grammarly and QuillBot are two of the most popular options — but they serve fundamentally different purposes. Grammarly excels at grammar checking, style improvement, and tone detection, while QuillBot specializes in paraphrasing and sentence rewriting. Choosing between them depends on whether you need a comprehensive writing editor or a powerful paraphrasing tool.

In this detailed comparison, I've tested both tools extensively with real-world writing projects to help you make the right choice.

| Best For | Writers, professionals, students needing comprehensive editing | Content creators, researchers, students needing paraphrasing |

What Is Grammarly?

Grammarly is a comprehensive AI-powered writing assistant that helps you write clear, mistake-free, and effective content. It checks your writing for grammar errors, spelling mistakes, punctuation issues, and style improvements in real-time.

Key Features:
  • Advanced Grammar Checking: Catches errors that basic spell-checkers miss
  • Style Suggestions: Improves clarity, conciseness, and readability
  • Tone Detection: Analyzes how your writing sounds to readers
  • Plagiarism Checker: Scans against 16 billion web pages (Premium)
  • Genre-Specific Writing Style: Tailors suggestions to your writing goal (email, essay, blog post, etc.)
  • Vocabulary Enhancement: Suggests stronger word choices
Best Use Cases:
  • Professional emails and business communication
  • Academic essays and research papers
  • Blog posts and content writing
  • Social media posts
  • Creative writing projects

What Is QuillBot?

QuillBot is an AI-powered paraphrasing tool that rewrites sentences and paragraphs while maintaining the original meaning. It's designed to help you rephrase content, improve fluency, and avoid plagiarism by rewriting text in different ways.

Key Features:
  • Paraphrasing Tool: 7 different modes (Standard, Fluency, Formal, Academic, Simple, Creative, Expand)
  • Summarizer: Condenses long text into key points
  • Grammar Checker: Basic grammar and spelling correction
  • Citation Generator: Creates citations in APA, MLA, Chicago styles
  • Plagiarism Checker: Scans for duplicate content (Premium)
  • Translator: Supports 30+ languages
Best Use Cases:
  • Paraphrasing research for academic papers
  • Rewriting content to avoid duplication
  • Simplifying complex sentences
  • Translating and paraphrasing foreign text
  • Creating multiple versions of marketing copy

Feature-by-Feature Comparison

1. Grammar and Spelling Checking

Grammarly: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Grammarly's grammar checking is industry-leading. It catches:

  • Subject-verb agreement errors
  • Comma splices and run-on sentences
  • Misplaced modifiers
  • Incorrect verb tenses
  • Contextual spelling errors (e.g., "there" vs. "their")
Testing Example:

Original: "The team are working on their project, however they haven't finished it yet."

Grammarly's suggestions:

  • "The team is working on their project; however, they haven't finished it yet." ✅
  • Catches subject-verb agreement error
  • Fixes comma splice with semicolon
  • Adds missing comma after "however"
QuillBot: ⭐⭐⭐

QuillBot's grammar checker catches basic errors but misses more nuanced issues:

  • Basic spelling mistakes ✅
  • Simple punctuation errors ✅
  • Subject-verb agreement ✅
  • Complex grammar issues ❌ (often missed)
  • Contextual errors ❌ (limited detection)
Winner: Grammarly — If grammar checking is your priority, Grammarly is significantly better.

2. Paraphrasing and Rewriting

Grammarly: ❌ Not Available

Grammarly does not have a dedicated paraphrasing feature. While it suggests sentence rewrites for clarity, it doesn't offer full-sentence paraphrasing.

QuillBot: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

QuillBot's paraphrasing is its core strength. It offers 7 modes:

1. Standard: Balanced changes (default)

2. Fluency: Improves flow and readability

3. Formal: Makes writing more professional

4. Academic: Scholarly tone for research papers

5. Simple: Uses simpler words and shorter sentences

6. Creative: More dramatic rewrites

7. Expand: Lengthens sentences (adds detail)

Testing Example:

Original: "Artificial intelligence is transforming the way businesses operate by automating repetitive tasks and providing data-driven insights."

QuillBot Results:
  • Standard: "Artificial intelligence is changing how businesses work by automating routine tasks and offering insights based on data."
  • Formal: "Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing business operations through the automation of repetitive tasks and the provision of data-driven insights."
  • Simple: "AI is changing how businesses work. It automates boring tasks and gives helpful information from data."
  • Creative: "The business world is being reshaped by artificial intelligence, which automates mundane activities and delivers analytics-powered wisdom."
Winner: QuillBot — No contest. QuillBot's paraphrasing is powerful and offers multiple tone options.

3. Tone and Style Analysis

Grammarly: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Grammarly's tone detector analyzes your writing and tells you how it sounds:

  • Tone Labels: Friendly, formal, confident, optimistic, urgent, etc.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Updates as you type
  • Goal-Based Suggestions: Tailors tone to audience (boss, colleague, friend)
Example: Writing "I need this done ASAP" reads as "urgent and direct" — Grammarly might suggest "Could you prioritize this task?" for a more polite tone.QuillBot: ❌ Not Available

QuillBot doesn't analyze tone. You can change tone via paraphrasing modes (Formal, Creative, Simple), but there's no tone detection.

Winner: Grammarly — Essential for professional communication.

4. Plagiarism Detection

Grammarly: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
  • Scans against 16 billion web pages
  • Highlights matched text with sources
  • Shows percentage of duplicate content
  • Available in Premium plan ($12/month)
QuillBot: ⭐⭐⭐⭐
  • Scans against billions of web pages
  • Shows matched sources
  • 20 pages/month on Premium plan ($9.95/month)
  • Unlimited scans on Premium Plus ($14.95/month)
Winner: Tie — Both are effective. QuillBot's Premium is cheaper, but Grammarly's Premium includes more features beyond plagiarism.

5. Browser Integration and Availability

Grammarly:
  • Browser extensions: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge
  • Desktop apps: Windows, Mac
  • Mobile apps: iOS, Android keyboard
  • Integrations: Google Docs, Microsoft Office, Gmail, Slack, LinkedIn
QuillBot:
  • Browser extensions: Chrome, Edge
  • Desktop apps: None (web-based only)
  • Mobile apps: iOS, Android (limited features)
  • Integrations: Google Docs, Microsoft Word
Winner: Grammarly — Better cross-platform support and deeper integrations.

6. Additional Features

Grammarly:
  • GrammarlyGO (Beta): AI writing assistant that generates text, rewrites sentences, and suggests ideas
  • Brand Tones: Set custom tone guidelines for teams (Business plan)
  • Style Guide: Customize rules for your organization
  • Performance Stats: Writing insights (word count, vocabulary, readability)
QuillBot:
  • Summarizer: Condenses articles and documents into key points
  • Citation Generator: Creates citations in multiple formats
  • Translator: Translates text in 30+ languages
  • Co-Writer: Integrated writing environment with paraphraser, summarizer, and grammar checker
Winner: Depends on your needs — Grammarly offers team collaboration features; QuillBot offers research-focused tools.

Real-World Testing: Side-by-Side Comparison

Original Draft:

However, many marketers don't fully understand how to use these tools effective. In this article, we'll explore the best AI marketing tools available in 2026 and how to use them to improve your marketing efforts."

Grammarly's Edits:

"Artificial intelligence has changed the marketing industry completely. Companies are now using AI tools to automate tasks, analyze data, and create content. However, many marketers don't fully understand how to use these tools effectively. In this article, we'll explore the best AI marketing tools available in 2026 and how to use them to improve your marketing efforts."

Changes Made:
  • Fixed subject-verb agreement ("have" → "has") ✅
  • Corrected verb forms ("for automating" → "to automate") ✅

Tone Detection: "Informative and confident"

"The marketing industry has been fundamentally transformed by artificial intelligence. Organizations currently employ AI-powered tools for task automation, data analysis, and content generation. Nevertheless, a significant number of marketers lack comprehensive understanding regarding the effective utilization of these technologies. This article examines the premier AI marketing solutions accessible in 2026 and provides guidance on leveraging them to enhance marketing initiatives."

Changes Made:
  • Improved formality and sophistication ✅
  • Varied sentence structure ✅
  • More professional vocabulary ✅
  • Fixed grammar errors in the process ✅

Which Tool Did Better?

  • For grammar correction: Grammarly caught and fixed all errors

FAQ

Is Grammarly better than QuillBot?

It depends on your needs. Grammarly is better for grammar checking, tone detection, and professional writing. QuillBot is better for paraphrasing, rewriting content, and academic research. For most users, Grammarly is the more versatile tool.

Can QuillBot replace Grammarly?

No. QuillBot's grammar checker is basic compared to Grammarly. While QuillBot excels at paraphrasing, it won't catch many grammar, style, and tone issues that Grammarly detects. Use QuillBot for rewriting and Grammarly for editing.

Is QuillBot worth paying for?

Yes, if you frequently paraphrase content. The Premium plan ($9.95/month) unlocks unlimited paraphrasing, 7 modes, and a plagiarism checker. The free plan (125 words/paraphrase, 3 modes) is functional but limiting for heavy users.

Does Grammarly have a paraphrasing tool?

No. Grammarly focuses on grammar, style, and tone. It suggests sentence rewrites for clarity but doesn't offer full-sentence paraphrasing like QuillBot. For paraphrasing, you'll need to use QuillBot or a tool like Wordtune.

Which is better for students?

For grammar and essay editing: Grammarly Premium (better tone and style feedback)For paraphrasing research sources: QuillBot Premium (avoid plagiarism, multiple modes)Best combo: QuillBot Free + Grammarly Premium (affordable and comprehensive)

Most students benefit from having both, but if you can only choose one, pick Grammarly if writing quality is your priority, or QuillBot if paraphrasing research is your main need.

Can I use both Grammarly and QuillBot together?

Yes! They're complementary tools:

1. Write your draft in Google Docs or Word

2. Use QuillBot to paraphrase complex sections or rewrite awkward sentences

3. Use Grammarly to check grammar, style, and tone

4. Publish your polished content

Many professional writers and students use both tools in their workflow.

Is Grammarly free good enough?

Grammarly Free is useful for basic grammar and spelling, but it's limited. You won't get:

  • Tone detection
  • Advanced style suggestions
  • Plagiarism checker
  • Vocabulary enhancement
  • Genre-specific writing style

For casual writing (texts, social media), Grammarly Free is sufficient. For professional or academic writing, Premium is worth $12/month.

Does QuillBot detect AI-generated content?

No. QuillBot is not an AI detection tool. If you need to check for AI-generated content, use tools like GPTZero, Originality.ai, or Turnitin. However, QuillBot can help you rewrite AI-generated text to make it sound more human.

Which tool is better for non-native English speakers?

Grammarly is significantly better for non-native speakers. Its grammar checking, tone detection, and style suggestions help improve English writing quality. QuillBot's paraphrasing can help, but it won't teach you why something is grammatically incorrect like Grammarly does.

Can QuillBot improve my writing skills?

QuillBot is a rewriting tool, not a teaching tool. While you can learn from its paraphrases, it doesn't explain grammar rules or style improvements like Grammarly does. For skill development, use Grammarly or ProWritingAid.
