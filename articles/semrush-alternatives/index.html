Why Look for Semrush Alternatives?

Common reasons to explore alternatives:

  • Price: $129.95/mo is too expensive
  • Simplicity: Semrush is overwhelming for beginners
  • Specialization: Need better backlinks (Ahrefs) or content (Frase)
  • Budget: Small businesses can't justify $1,559/year
  • Features: Need only keyword research, not full suite

Whatever your reason, here are the best Semrush alternatives ranked by use case.

How to Choose the Right Alternative

  • Backlink analysis is your #1 priority

Choose Moz Pro if:

  • You're new to SEO
  • You want simpler, easier-to-learn tools
  • Local SEO matters (Moz Local)

Choose Ubersuggest if:

  • Budget is your #1 constraint ($29/mo)
  • You want a lifetime deal ($290 one-time)
  • Basic SEO tools are enough

Choose SE Ranking if:

  • You want Semrush features at 50% cost
  • White-label reports matter (agencies)
  • You need all-in-one on a budget
