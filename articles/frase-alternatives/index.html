Why Look for Frase Alternatives?

Frase is an excellent SEO content tool, but it's not perfect for everyone. Common reasons to explore alternatives:

  • Article limits: Solo plan restricts to 10 articles/month
  • AI writing quality: Competitors like Jasper produce better first drafts
  • Enterprise features: Frase lacks advanced reporting and team collaboration
  • Technical SEO: Tools like Semrush offer broader SEO capabilities
  • Pricing: Some alternatives offer better value at scale

Whatever your reason, here are the best Frase alternatives ranked by use case.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest Frase alternative?

NeuronWriter at $23/month offers the best value, providing similar features to Frase's $45 Basic plan. For pure SEO optimization without AI writing, PageOptimizer Pro starts at $34/month.

Which Frase alternative has the best AI writing?

INK and Scalenut both have superior AI writing compared to Frase. If AI writing is your priority, consider pairing Surfer SEO (for optimization) with Jasper or Copy.ai (for writing).

Can I use free alternatives to Frase?

MarketMuse offers a limited free plan (10 queries/month). For completely free options, use ChatGPT for content generation and Google Search Console for basic SEO data, though you'll miss out on integrated optimization scoring.

Which tool is best for agencies?

Surfer SEO (white-label reports) or Clearscope (enterprise features) are best for agencies. Frase Team plan is also solid at $115/month for unlimited articles.

Do I need Frase if I have Semrush?

Not necessarily. Semrush includes a Writing Assistant that covers basic content optimization. However, Frase's automated brief generation and content-first workflow may still be valuable. Many users subscribe to both.

Which alternative works best with WordPress?

Frase, Surfer SEO, and Semrush all have WordPress plugins. Frase's one-click publishing is the smoothest, but Surfer's Chrome extension works well in WordPress block editor.

Is Clearscope worth 10x the price of Frase?

For enterprise teams with budget, yes. Clearscope's reporting, keyword research, and UX are superior. For freelancers and small businesses, Frase or NeuronWriter offer better ROI.
