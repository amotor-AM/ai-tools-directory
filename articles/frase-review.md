## What is Frase?

Frase is an all-in-one SEO content optimization tool that combines SERP research, AI-powered writing, and content optimization into a single workflow. Founded in 2016 and headquartered in Boston, Frase has become a go-to solution for content marketers who want to create search-optimized content faster.

The platform's core strength lies in its ability to analyze top-ranking Google results for any keyword and automatically generate content briefs with recommended headings, questions, keywords, and statistics. This research-first approach helps writers create content that's aligned with what Google already ranks highly.

### How Frase Works

1. **Research Phase:** Enter your target keyword, and Frase scrapes the top 20 Google results
2. **Brief Creation:** Automatically generates an outline with headings, questions, and key topics
3. **AI Writing:** Use AI templates to draft sections or full articles
4. **Optimization:** Real-time content score shows how well your content matches top-ranking pages
5. **Publishing:** Export to Google Docs, WordPress, or copy to your CMS

## Key Features

### 1. SERP Analysis & Topic Modeling

Frase's SERP analyzer is the foundation of its SEO workflow. Enter any keyword, and it:

- Scrapes top 20 Google results for that query
- Extracts common headings, questions, and topics
- Identifies keyword frequency and semantic relationships
- Analyzes content structure (word count, reading level, sentiment)
- Shows what competitors are ranking for

This data is presented in clean visualizations that make it easy to identify content gaps and opportunities.

### 2. Automated Content Briefs

One of Frase's standout features is automated brief generation. Instead of manually researching competitors and creating outlines, Frase does it in seconds:

- Suggested headings (H2s, H3s) from top-ranking content
- "People Also Ask" questions from Google
- Statistics and data points mentioned by competitors
- Recommended word count based on SERP averages
- Internal linking opportunities

Content briefs can be saved as templates and shared with writers, making it ideal for agencies managing multiple clients.

### 3. AI Content Generation

Frase includes AI writing capabilities powered by GPT models:

- **Article Writer:** Generate full articles from a keyword and outline
- **Paragraph Generator:** Write specific sections based on headings
- **Answer Generator:** Create FAQ answers from SERP data
- **Blog Intro/Outro:** Quick templates for opening and closing sections
- **Rewrite Tool:** Rephrase existing content to avoid duplication

The AI is trained to prioritize SEO-relevant content, pulling from top-ranking pages rather than generic knowledge.

### 4. Content Optimization Score

As you write, Frase displays a real-time optimization score (0-100) based on:

- Keyword usage (primary and LSI keywords)
- Topic coverage (semantic relevance)
- Heading structure (H1-H6 hierarchy)
- Content length
- Readability

The score updates live, helping you hit SEO targets before publishing.

### 5. Answer Engine

Frase's Answer Engine is designed for creating FAQ content and featured snippet opportunities:

- Scrapes "People Also Ask" questions from Google
- Generates concise answers (40-60 words)
- Optimized for featured snippet format
- Bulk answer generation for FAQ pages

This is particularly useful for targeting question-based keywords and zero-click searches.

### 6. Integrations

- **Google Docs:** Export optimized content directly
- **WordPress:** One-click publishing with formatting preserved
- **Google Search Console:** Import keyword and traffic data
- **Zapier:** Connect to 3,000+ apps for workflow automation

### What Frase Does Best

Frase cuts SERP analysis time from hours to minutes. The automated brief generation alone justifies the subscription for busy content teams.

**2. Align Content with Search Intent**  
By analyzing what already ranks, Frase helps you create content that matches user intent and Google's preferences.

**3. Affordable Entry Point**  
At $15/month, Frase is accessible to individual writers and small businesses who can't afford $100+/month tools.

**4. All-in-One Workflow**  
Research, write, and optimize in one platform without juggling multiple tools.

### Where Frase Falls Short

**1. AI Writing Quality**  
While useful for drafts, Frase's AI output isn't as polished as dedicated writing tools like Jasper or Copy.ai. Expect to edit heavily.

**2. Limited Creative Use Cases**  
Frase is built for SEO content (blogs, articles, guides). It's not ideal for social media, ad copy, or creative storytelling.

**3. No Plagiarism Checker**  
Unlike competitors like Surfer SEO, Frase doesn't include built-in plagiarism detection. You'll need a separate tool like Copyscape.

**4. Article Limits**  
The Solo and Basic plans restrict how many optimization documents you can create monthly, which can be limiting for high-volume teams.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Frase worth it for beginners?

Yes, especially at the $15/month Solo plan. Frase simplifies SEO research and helps beginners understand what makes content rank without needing deep SEO expertise.

### Can Frase replace my SEO tool?

Not entirely. Frase excels at content optimization but lacks comprehensive features like backlink analysis, site audits, and advanced keyword research. It works best alongside tools like Ahrefs or Semrush for keyword discovery.

### How accurate is the content optimization score?

Frase's score is based on correlation, not causation. A high score doesn't guarantee rankings, but it does ensure your content covers topics and keywords that top-ranking pages include. Treat it as a guideline, not a guarantee.

### Does Frase work for languages other than English?

Yes, Frase supports 30+ languages for AI writing and SERP analysis. However, optimization quality is best for English, Spanish, French, and German.

### Can I use Frase for existing content?

Absolutely. Many users import existing articles into Frase to identify content gaps and improve optimization scores before republishing.

### Is there a free trial?

Frase offers a 7-day money-back guarantee instead of a traditional free trial. This gives you a week to test all features risk-free.

### How many articles can I optimize per month?

- Solo: 10 articles/month
- Basic: 30 articles/month
- Team: Unlimited articles

Research and SERP analysis don't count against your limit—only content optimization documents.

### Does Frase integrate with WordPress?

Yes, Frase has a WordPress plugin that allows one-click publishing directly from the editor with formatting preserved.
