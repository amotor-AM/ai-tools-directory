The Honest Truth About This Comparison

Let's be blunt: Clearscope is 11x more expensive than Frase ($170/mo vs. $15/mo entry plans).

This isn't a fair comparison for most users. Clearscope targets enterprise teams with $5,000+ monthly content budgets. Frase targets freelancers, bloggers, and small agencies optimizing on a budget.

The real question isn't "Which is better?" but "Which is better for YOU?"

When Clearscope Makes Sense (Rare)

You should consider Clearscope if all of these are true:

  1. You're an enterprise content team (5+ writers)
  2. You have a monthly content budget over $5,000
  3. You create 20+ high-stakes articles per month (enterprise SEO, thought leadership)
  4. You need advanced reporting for stakeholders
  5. Content quality is your #1 priority (over quantity)
  6. You value premium UX and customer support
  7. Price is not a primary concern

If fewer than 5 of these apply, choose Frase.

When Frase Makes Sense (Most Users)

You should choose Frase if any of these are true:

  1. You're a freelancer, blogger, or small business
  2. Budget matters (you're not spending $200/mo on a single tool)
  3. You need AI writing assistance
  4. You want automated content brief generation
  5. You create 10-50 articles per month
  6. You're new to SEO content optimization
  7. You want all-in-one research + writing + optimization

If 3+ of these apply, Frase is your best bet.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Clearscope really 11x better than Frase?

No. Clearscope is perhaps 10-20% better in UX, keyword research, and optimization accuracy. It's not 11x better. The price premium is for enterprise features (reporting, support, collaboration) that most small teams don't need.

Should I upgrade from Frase to Clearscope?

Only if:

  1. You're an enterprise team (10+ people)
  2. Budget isn't a concern ($2,000+/mo for content tools)
  3. You need advanced reporting for executives
  4. You've outgrown Frase's collaboration features

For 95% of users, Frase is sufficient.

Can I get Clearscope-level quality with Frase?

Yes, with workflow optimization:

  1. Use Frase for content briefs and optimization
  2. Add Ahrefs or Semrush for superior keyword research ($99-129/mo)
  3. Use Jasper or Claude for better AI writing ($20-59/mo)
  4. Total: $134-228/mo for best-in-class workflow (still cheaper than Clearscope)

Does Clearscope have a free trial?

No. Clearscope offers demos but no free trial. Frase offers a 7-day money-back guarantee.

Which tool has better customer support?

Clearscope -- dedicated account managers, faster response times, proactive tips. However, Frase's email support is adequate for most users.

Can small businesses afford Clearscope?

Rarely. At $170/mo minimum ($2,040/year), Clearscope is a significant expense for small businesses. Most are better served by Frase ($15-45/mo).
