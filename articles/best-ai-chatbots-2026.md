## FAQ

### Which AI chatbot is the smartest?

**Claude 3.5 Sonnet** is the most capable for complex reasoning, coding, and analysis. **ChatGPT** is close behind and more versatile.

### Which AI chatbot is completely free?

**Pi** offers full access with no paid tier. **ChatGPT's free tier** (GPT-3.5) is also excellent. **Perplexity** offers 5 Pro searches/day for free.
